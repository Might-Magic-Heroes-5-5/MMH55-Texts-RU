All Elves worship the ideal of Harmony, Alaron did so to the point that he even viewed his enemies as necessary and worthy, leading to a renowned reluctance to strike the decisive blow against a weakened enemy. This reluctance has been fatal for King Alexei Griffin IV as he charged into the gates of Sheogh but his elven and wizard allies failed to follow. Ever since, Alaron has been consumed by guilt, and is now willing to do what is necessary to avoid the history to repeat itself.
